About ProcastFlow

ProcastFlow is a frontend-first productivity platform that uses simulated AI logic to help you plan and execute work without delay.

What is ProcastFlow?

ProcastFlow is a lightweight productivity workspace that turns procrastination into momentum. It offers a simple landing experience and a dashboard built for task planning, calendar organization, and progress tracking.

AI-based productivity concept

While ProcastFlow does not connect to a backend AI service, it mimics smart behavior in the browser: breaking tasks into action steps, organizing dates automatically, and generating productivity insights from your task history.

Work at your pace

PCF keeps the interface clean so you can move quickly from planning to doing, avoiding distraction and staying focused on real progress.
